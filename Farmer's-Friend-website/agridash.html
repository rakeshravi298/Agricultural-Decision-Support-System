Crop suggestion dashboard

This dashboard can assist you to choose the crops that can be harvested in your district and season.

Please select the State you want to harvest your crops from the given filter drop down. Also select the district and seasons from the filter dropdown menu. Ensure that you give all the values correctly.

Data has been obtained for each and every district and we have the crop's yearly historic data.

In the bar graph the crops are in the rows and the average of the crop's yields are calculated and represent the length of the bars. The Pie chart represents the crop's area proportion in the given district... Higher the area's percentage more farmers are planting the crop in your district. The wordcloud has all the crops in your district and sized by the Production values. Bigger the word higher the production.
